🚨 Critical Findings

🔴 NOT INDEXED: Google returns zero results for site:qbsglobal.ae. The site is completely invisible in organic search. This is the #1 blocker.
🔴 CONTENT DESERT: Only 8 pages in sitemap (homepage, blog index, 1 blog post, VegaMind landing, login page, + Arabic mirrors). AI engines need 20+ authoritative pages for citation eligibility.
🔴 4 H1 TAGS ON HOMEPAGE: The homepage has 4 separate H1 headings. Google expects exactly 1 H1 per page. This confuses search engines about the page's primary topic.
🔴 ZERO BRAND PRESENCE: "QBS Global" and "VegaMind" return zero relevant results in Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, or any AI engine. The brand has no digital footprint outside its own domain.
🟠 MISSING META DESCRIPTIONS: Blog index and VegaMind landing page have no meta descriptions. Only the blog article has a proper one.
🟠 NO FAQ SCHEMA: VegaMind has a FAQ section but no FAQPage JSON-LD schema. This is a missed opportunity for both Google rich results and AI answer extraction.
🟠 NO LLMS.TXT: No llms.txt file exists to help AI crawlers understand the site and its offerings.
🟡 THIN INTERNAL LINKING: With only 8 pages and minimal cross-linking, search engines can't build a topic graph for the domain.

Content Structure Template (Answer-First)

Every content piece should follow this structure for maximum AI citation probability:

1. Answer ParagraphFirst 2-3 sentences directly answer the query. Include primary keyword. This is what AI extracts.
2. Key Facts TableNumbers, thresholds, costs in a structured HTML table. Easy for snippets and AI.
3. Detailed SectionsH2/H3 sections expanding the answer. Cite authoritative sources. Target secondary keywords.
4. Step-by-StepNumbered steps with HowTo schema. AI extracts procedures from numbered lists.
5. FAQ Section5-8 related questions with FAQPage JSON-LD. Target long-tail question keywords.
6. Internal LinksLink to related hub content. Builds topical authority for the domain.
7. CTATie educational content to QBS Global services. "Book a Meeting" or "Free Audit" offer.

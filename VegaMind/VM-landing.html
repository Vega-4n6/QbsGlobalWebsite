FAQ

Questions people
actually ask.

Do I need to know how to code?
+
Absolutely not. If you can copy and paste text, you can install VegaMind. One line of code goes on your website — that's it. You manage everything else from a dashboard, no coding required.
What happens when I hit my conversation limit?
+
You'll get an email warning at 80% usage. When the limit is reached, the bot pauses until your billing cycle resets. You can also upgrade your plan at any time to get more conversations immediately.
Can I put it on multiple websites?
+
Yes! You can create separate bots for each website, each with its own name, color, personality, and welcome message. The Orbit plan supports 5 bots, and Nova is unlimited.
Will it clash with my website's design?
+
Never. VegaMind is fully isolated — its styles live in their own namespace and won't affect a single pixel of your existing site. You customise the color and position to match your brand.
How does the bot know what to say?
+
Your bot connects to your n8n or custom AI workflow — so it can be trained on your specific products, FAQs, and business rules. On the Nova plan, we help you set this up end-to-end.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes, any time. No long contracts, no cancellation fees. You can cancel from your dashboard in 30 seconds. Your bot will stay active until the end of your current billing period.
